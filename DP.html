Dual Point Troubleshooting

Reboot the controller.

The Dual Point controller is a small computer in the tech closet or mounted on the wall in the office. It is either a Dell or HP thin client. Press the power button once to shut down the controller. Once the lights on the controller are off, press the power button again to bring it back up.
