KVS Troubleshooting

Reboot the KVS controller.

  1. The KVS controller is a small computer mounted behind the monitor.
    • KVS 1 - Behind side 1 lunch monitor.
    • KVS 2 - Behind side 2 breakfast monitor OR behind DT expo monitor
    • KVS 3 - Behind FC expo monitor
    • KVS 4 (CBB) - Mounted on the wall in the DT present booth
    • KVS 4 (BDAP) - Behind one of the DT present monitors
    • KVS 5 - HOTG controller
    • KVS 6&7 (BDAP) - Behind each monitor in BDAP
  2. Press the power button once and wait for the controller to shut down.
  3. Press the power button again to boot up the KVS controller.

KVS Troubleshooting

Follow this temporary workaround:

  1. Connect a keyboard to one of the front USB ports on the KVS.
  2. Use the following commands on the keyboard:
    • A - Serve
    • C - Next Order
    • E - Side ON
    • J - Side OFF
