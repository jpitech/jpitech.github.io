Biometrics Troubleshooting

Clean the fingerprint reader.

Use a piece of clear Scotch tape to clean the fingerprint reader. DO NOT use anything other than a piece of tape to clean the fingerprint reader. Any liquids or chemicals may damage the fingerprint reader.

Biometrics Troubleshooting

Reboot the register.

Press the power button once and allow the register to shut down. Press the power button again to boot the register back up. DO NOT press and hold the power button to shut down the register. Data corruption may occur if this method is used.

Biometrics Troubleshooting

Re-enroll your finger scan.

On the back office server, open the McDonald's Biometrics application. Log in using your time card number. Follow the directions to re-enroll your finger scan. NOTE: Finger scans expire one year after creating the finger scan record. You will need to re-enroll your finger after a year has passed.

After re-enrolling your finger scan, test the new scan by accessing the manager menu on the register.
