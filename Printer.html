Correct the cabling on the printer.

  1. Reseat the cables on the printer.
  2. Reseat the USB cable on the register.
  3. If the cable is not connected to the red USB port on the register, move the cable to the red USB port.
  4. Attempt to reprint a receipt to test.

Printer Troubleshooting

Check the data cables on the A/B switch box.

Make sure the data cables are connected to the correct ports. If they are not labeled, or if you're uncomfortable checking these, contact your OTP.

  • KVS controllers should be connected to the I/O port.
  • Lunch printers should be connected to the A port.
  • Breakfast printers should be connected to the B port.
